Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 3/4 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 2 cups chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Sift flour, baking soda, and salt together; set aside.
  2. Beat brown sugar, melted butter, and white sugar until smooth.
  3. Beat in egg, egg yolk, and vanilla until creamy; add flour mixture and stir until combined.
  4. Mix in flour, then chocolate chips.
  5. Drop spoonfuls of dough onto baking sheets.
  6. Bake for 10 minutes or until golden.
